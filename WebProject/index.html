ABOUT US

At THE BRIDGE our goal is simple – to make our customers’ outsourcing experience as easy and hassle-free as possible.

We achieve this by continually investing in the latest manufacturing technologies and techniques, ensuring that regardless of the complexity or scale of the assembly, we are equipped to handle it. Utilising our extensive Supply Chain Management experience and network we ensure there is visibility on lead times and market availability. We also propose component alternatives where possible to improve lead times and/or costs. Our highly skilled engineering team have a keen eye for detail and are on hand to assist from the outset of each project. We provide DFM reporting for every assembly we build. Specific PFMEA and IQ, OQ & PQ reporting can be completed where required. With tailored solutions to suit your needs, it doesn’t matter whether you are looking to outsource prototyping, low, medium, or high-volume projects. If you are looking for a full turnkey solution, partial turnkey or want to free-issue the parts, we can handle it. From PCBA though to full box build and distribution, Smart Electronics can provide a tailored solution to suit your needs.

Capability

We manufacture quick turnaround prototypes and low to high-volume production runs. Our electronic manufacturing capabilities combined with highly skilled operators, technicians and engineers consistently deliver the highest quality products and services for a diverse customer base within all sectors of industry

We have extensive SMT capabilities in-house with all 3 of our SMT lines having been purchased since 2019, giving us full flexibility to run any product on any line.

We have a dedicated SMT line for Prototyping to ensure there is no disruption to our main lines and production schedule when prototype builds are required.

Our 2 Semi-Automated Selective Soldering machines are perfect for when larger production quantities are required. Selective Soldering gives added benefits over the more traditional Wave Soldering technique by enabling us to select the specific PCB area that requires soldering. So even double-sided boards are easily soldered at scale with consistency

Welcome to THE BRIDGE career

At THE BRIDGE our goal is simple – to make our customers’ outsourcing experience as easy and hassle-free as possible. To achieve this we empower our staff, enabling them to make a difference.

Founded in 2006 Smart Electronics Ltd has continually grown to establish itself as one of Irelands leading contract electronic manufacturers

Our Success has been built on our team and our commitment to utilising the latest technologies when building high quality, high tech PCBA’s and other electro-mechanical assemblies for a variety of different sectors. We have the following opportunities available to join the Smart Electronics team.

Manufacturing Process Engineer

We are looking for a Manufacturing Process Engineer to join our expanding Engineering team.

Reporting to the Operations Manager the Manufacturing Process Engineer is part of the team, which is responsible for ensuring the efficient and effective operation of the manufacturing plant.

Materials Manager

We are looking for a Materials Manager to join our senior management team.

Reporting to the Managing Director the Materials Manager will be responsible for managing a mix of supply chain activities. They will be tasked with determining and deploying effective strategies to maintain and improve the supply and control of materials.

General Operator

We are looking for a general operator to join our expanding production team.

Reporting to the production supervisor a general operator is part of the team, which is responsible for efficiently and effectively assembling production work orders to meet the production schedule and targets.

Surface Mount Operator

We are looking for a surface mount operator to join our expanding production team.

Reporting to the production supervisor a surface mount operator is part of the team, which is responsible for efficiently and effectively running our automated production lines to meet the production schedule and targets.
